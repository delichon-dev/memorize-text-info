# Memorize Text

A privacy-first iOS app for practicing learning a text by heart through repeated recall.

This app is currently in TestFlight beta testing.

## Privacy
- No accounts
- No tracking
- No analytics
- No data collection

All data is stored locally on your device.

Read the full privacy policy here: [Privacy Policy](privacy).
